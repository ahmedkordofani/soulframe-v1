# SoulFrame

**AI Music Humanization Workflow Prototype**

SoulFrame is a creative technology prototype designed to help producers review AI-generated music drafts, identify synthetic artifacts, map revision priorities, and prepare cleaner client update notes.

The goal is simple:

> Help AI-generated music feel more human, emotional, and intentional.

---

## Live Demo

https://soulframe-v1.vercel.app

---

## What SoulFrame Does

SoulFrame is built around a practical AI-humanization workflow:

1. Review an AI-generated music draft
2. Identify synthetic or unnatural-sounding elements
3. Map the main revision priorities
4. Compare draft progress through a before/after workflow
5. Generate clear client-facing update notes

## Why This Exists

AI-generated music can be fast and impressive, but it often lacks the subtle details that make music feel alive.

SoulFrame explores how producers can use AI as a tool without removing the human ear, emotional judgement, and creative decision-making from the process.

This prototype is part of my wider work as **ChordOfAnnie**, focused on humanizing AI-generated sound and bringing soul back into music.

---

## Current Version

**V1: Workflow Prototype**

This version uses simulated analysis and structured example reports. It does not yet perform real audio analysis.

The purpose of V1 is to define the workflow, interface, terminology, and user experience before moving into deeper audio processing.
